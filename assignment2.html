STATS FROM THE GAME

JUVENTUS - TORINO | SERIE A

Other than Alex Sandro's 300th game in all competitions for Juventus and Wojciech Szczesny's 200th game - as well as his 100th victory in Serie A with Juve, the roller-coaster 4-2 win over Torino provided a series of statistics and curiosities. Four goals in the first half between Juventus and Torino for the first time since 25th January 1995 indicated it was to be a match to remember.

300 appearances with the shirt of Juventus. Alex Sandro joined an exclusive club of players that have made turned out in our colors three hundred times.
